About Me

The emergence of Web3 allowed me to take a year off, and really figure out what I wanted in life. Freedom and purpose are truly what I see. I love to travel and meet new people. I need purpose, and building brings that to me. Both digital and physical design and infrasture have always interested me deeply, but I've found over the years that I enjoy building software much more than buildings or hardware.

I found a love for writing software when I was in my senior year of highschool. My interest in the ocean and fluid dynamics drove me to pursue Mechananical Engineering, where I was able to leverage my love for writing code. Since then, I've never stopped writing or building -- except for a few vacations :)
